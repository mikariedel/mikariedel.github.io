About Mika Riedel

  • Mika Riedel is a visual artist who studied painting and sculpting at Japan University College of Art. She taught art and pottery in Japan, USA, and the UK. Mika now lives in London where she enjoys galleries, museums, books, podcasts, music, running, meditating and dancing.
